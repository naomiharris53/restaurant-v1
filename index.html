History and info

It all began on Febuary 17 1993, when Kent Taylor opened the first Texas Roadhouse in Clarksville, Idianna. Kent's goal was to own not just a family restaurant and not just a steak restaurant, but a place where everyone, of all ages could come and have a delicious meal and some. fun for a great price. We like to brag about our hand-cut steaks, Fall-off-the-bone ribs. made-from-scratch-sides,and fresh-baked bread.Everything we do goes into making our hearty meals that stand out. We handcraft mostly everything we serve.We provide larger portions so you can get more food for your dollar.In addition we have ice-cold beers or legendary margarita to wash it all down. The food is not the only thing thats the best here, our fun is too. We have line dancers and jukebox, free to do and use for our friendly customers.For sure you will enjoy every moment you spend at Texas Roadhouse.Our team definitely has an incredible sense of pride in everything they do. Our passion ensures you to have a legendary experience. Because of our passion for our guest, our team members are involved outside the restaurant in the local communites as a way to give back.
